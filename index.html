About Tangled Synapses Aviation

Since the 2014 Brazil World Cup, 2018 Russia World Cup, and 2022 Qatar World Cup, Tangled Synapses Aviation has been flying soccer fans and national teams to this global event. In 2026, we will be flying fans and teams from around the world to host cities across Mexico, Canada and the United States.
